About TSR

We are a non-profit organization willing to take the initiative to educate our generation. Our passion for coding has driven us to assist younger students to develop their interest in this vast field of technology. Our goal is to create a safe and efficient learning environment that allows our students to succeed and become benificial members of society tomorrow. By doing so, we are helping the future generation innovate new pieces of technology.

Vision

Desired Outcome

We strive to make our students and community feel welcome into the field of Computer Science. Students are often not challenged enough in the standard school curriculum, and thus we've formed this enrichment club. We hope to see an increment in interest of computer science in the coming generations.
